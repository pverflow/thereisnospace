Audi Nrburgring Nordschleife Digital Twin

Project Overview

Our team successfully delivered a highly accurate digital representation of the legendary Nrburgring Nordschleife track in Germany for AUDI's dynamic driving simulator. This challenging project required exceptional precision and technical expertise to recreate the world's longest permanent racetrack for professional driver training and vehicle development purposes.

The application renders across 10 screens (7 main projectors and 3 rearview mirror displays) running at 120Hz using Unity's High Definition Render Pipeline (HDRP), providing an immersive and realistic driving experience for AUDI's engineering and training programs.

Technical Achievements

  • Track Precision: 21 kilometers of track recreated with 10cm accuracy
  • Corner Mapping: 150 corners with accurate turn-in markers for drivers
  • Terrain Coverage: 6.5 km of detailed terrain modeling
  • Environmental Assets: 40,000 trees and bushes strategically placed
  • Signage System: Over 500 unique signs textured and positioned around the track
  • Performance Optimization: Stable 120Hz rendering across 10 synchronized displays

My Responsibilities

  • Technical Art direction and pipeline development
  • Asset optimization for high-frequency rendering
  • Environmental placement and terrain modeling workflows
  • Performance profiling and optimization for multi-screen setup
  • Quality assurance and accuracy validation
  • Cross-team coordination with AUDI engineering requirements

Technical Implementation

  • Engine: Unity 3D with High Definition Render Pipeline (HDRP)
  • Rendering: 10-screen synchronized display at 120Hz
  • Precision Mapping: GPS and LiDAR data integration for track accuracy
  • Asset Pipeline: Optimized LOD systems for seamless performance
  • Environmental Systems: Procedural vegetation placement and optimization
  • Quality Standards: Sub-centimeter accuracy requirements

Project Impact

Successfully delivered a mission-critical simulation environment for AUDI's advanced driver training and vehicle development programs. The digital twin of the Nrburgring Nordschleife enables engineers and drivers to experience the world's most challenging race track in a controlled, repeatable environment, supporting both vehicle testing and professional driver education initiatives.
